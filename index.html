Welcome to my page! A bit about me!

Greetings! My name is Joaquin De Losada, and I am a 23-year-old Peruvian enthusiastic about Game Development and AI Systems in video games. Recently, I completed my undergraduate studies at Brock University, majoring in Interactive Arts and Science with a minor in Computer Science. While at university, I engaged in various projects that ignited my passion for game development and AI systems, which I intend to pursue in my future career. Some of the projects I've worked on can be found on this website, as they demonstrate my programming and game development skillset.

AI Related Projects

The following section is a collection of the AI-related project I've worked on over the past few years. The AI I have the greatest passion for working on is NPC systems and gameplay-related AI systems. I started working on AI systems to understand how AI systems work in strategy games like Age of Empires 2 and a youtube channel called "AI and Games." Even though I am early in my career, I have some project ideas revolving around a behavior tree in Unity and procedurally generated puzzle games.

Finite State Machine (FSM) NPC in Unity

This project arises from a passion I have gained in the past few years around understanding and working on AI NPC systems for games. During this time, I've better understood how systems like Finite State Machines, Behavior Trees, and others have evolved over the years and shaped the gaming industry. After some time, I decided that the best course of action as a programmer would be implementing some of the systems myself. The main plan was to spend some months designing and making an AI NPC system. Allowing me to understand better the process that AI/Gameplay programmers and designers would go through when working on the AI systems for a game.
